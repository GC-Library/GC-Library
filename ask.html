The library is open online! While the library’s physical space is inaccessible due to COVID-19, many resources and services are available online. For updates, subscribe to the library blog and follow us on Twitter.

Worried about items you currently have checked out? CUNY libraries have suspended regular overdue fines as well as fines for recalled items. Also, all CUNY libraries now allow at least nine renewals of circulating items and will automatically renew items checked out to you. Additionally, interlibrary loan staff will automatically request renewals of ILL items on your behalf.

24/7 Chat

Our online chat service is available 24/7 and will be answered by a CUNY librarian, if available. When CUNY librarians are not available, an academic librarian from one of our partner libraries will assist you.

Submit a Question

Use this form to query a GC librarian, or email ref@gc.cuny.edu.

Research Consultations

Schedule a one-on-one research consultation with your subject librarian by filling out the appointment request form.

CUNY Account Information

The library’s CUNY Accounts page rounds up the various kinds of CUNY accounts and what they’re for.

Databases

Access any of the library’s 350 databases remotely, using your GC network credentials.

Journals

Use the journal title search tool to check whether the library has access to a particular journal through our databases. 

E-books

The library provides access to thousands of e-books, accessible through a variety of databases. Consult our e-books guide for more information.

Research Guides

GC librarians have created research guides for many academic disciplines, as well as guides on topics such as open educational resources (OER), data management, and using Zotero.

Interlibrary Loan

Interlibrary loan is available for copies of journal articles and book chapters, but requests for books and other physical items cannot currently be processed.

Online Workshops

Library workshops will be offered as webinars. Keep track of library events on the library calendar.

Research at NYPL

New York Public Library also offers remote access a wide variety of resources and services.
