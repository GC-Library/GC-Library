There are many ways to get help from a librarian. Choose the method that suits your needs:

Submit a question

Use this form to query a GC reference librarian. Answers will be sent during Reference Desk hours. For immediate assistance, use the 24/7 reference chat.

Ask a reference librarian

Visit the reference desk

The 2nd floor Reference Desk is staffed by librarians to provide research assistance on a drop-in basis. No appointment necessary.

View Reference Desk hours

Schedule a consultation

Schedule a one-on-one research consultation with your subject librarian by filling out the appointment request form.

Request an appointment

Suggest materials

Have a suggestion for our collection? We welcome suggestions for books and other materials.

Suggest new materials

Chat with a librarian

Our online chat service is available 24/7 and will be answered by a CUNY librarian, if available. During late-night hours, an academic librarian from one of our partner libraries may assist you.

Request a Class Visit

Invite a librarian to visit your class for an instruction session to introduce library research, demonstrate specific databases and tools, and help students hone their search techniques.

Request a visit
